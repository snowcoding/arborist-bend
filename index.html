Services

Text or call us today at (541) 480-5358 for a free estimate!

Tree trim and prune

Tree trimming, pruning and deadwooding are extremely important. We carefully prune and remove dead, broken, diseased and dying branches. Our careful pruning improves tree health as well as tree shape.

Tree and stump removal

We remove dead or diseased trees. And, if desired, we will grind the stump to eliminate any trip hazard and facilitate grass regeneration. Stump removal also prevents the tree from re-sprouting.

Broken limbs and fallen trees

We’re here for all your tree emergencies. We remove dead or diseased trees, cracked or broken limbs, cut and haul fallen trees. Sean S. Shriver Tree Care can often have the job completed same day if desired.

Fire prevention around your home

Let Sean S. Shriver Tree Care safeguard your home from wildfire with proven fire prevention techniques. Text or call for an appointment today.

Roof clearance

Sometimes it’s necessary to cut branches back from the roof. This prevents structural damage and limits pest infestation and fire hazards.

Mulching

Mulching is great for your trees and plants. It saves water, provides valuable nutrients and protects your plants from harmful landscape machinery.

Storm clean up

We’re there for you when disaster strikes. We remove fallen trees and debris from buildings, cars and homes.
